Parent-Staff Association

The aims of our Parent Staff Association are to organise events for the children and their families, to raise funds for additional resources and to help promote the school within the wider community.

We do not have an elected PSA committee. Any parent who wishes to become involved can attend our meetings at any time. The group consists of the Principal, Vice-Principal, teacher representatives and around fifteen enthusiastic parents. Meetings are held twice each term and last no more than one hour.
