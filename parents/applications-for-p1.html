Applications for P1

Do you have a child who is due to begin Primary 1 in September 2018?

Come along to our open night on Tuesday the 28th of November to have a look around the school and chat to the principal and teachers at Lisnasharragh!

Applications for Primary 1 will be available soon.


Admissions criteria

The Board of Governors draws up the admissions criteria and delegates the responsibility of its application to the Principal and senior clerical staff.

For more information on the admissions criteria please read the document below.



  Download PDF


Key dates

Each year the Department of Education permits Lisnasharragh to admit up to 60 children into P1. This admissions figure varies from school to school and is largely determined by the size of the school building. (A total of 58 children started P1 in September 2016.) We currently have two classes in the P1, P2, P3, P4 and P5 year groups.


Friday 25th Nov

Application Packs including a Prospectus and Enrolment Form will be available from Mrs Childs (our school secretary) from this date onwards. Enrolment Forms should be returned to our the school office. A birth certificate will be required.


Open Week: Monday 27 Nov to Friday 1 Dec

Prospective parents can arrange to have a walk round the school with the Principal (takes around 30 mins) and ask any questions that they might have.


Open Night (from 5:30PM): Tuesday 28 Nov

All prospective parents can come along and see the facilities and resources that the school has to offer. The Principals Talk will be at 6.00PM in the Parents Room - repeated at 7.00PM. Everyone is very welcome to attend - you will have the opportunity to hear our superb school choir singing their Christmas carols and songs at 7.40PM.


Christmas Activity Morning (10.00AM-12.30PM): Saturday 2 Dec

There will be lots of craft (and other) activities for children of all ages. Santa will also be making an appearance! Entrance and all craft activities are free. This is always a fun morning - everyone is welcome to come along!


Friday 5 Jan

A repeat of the Principals Talk at 9.20am (for those parents who were unable to attend on Open Night).


Wednesday 10 Jan

This is the final date by which all Enrolment Forms must be returned to Lisnasharragh Primary School (by 12 noon) - it is important not to be late!


Thursday 26 April

We will send you a letter confirming that your son or daughter has a place in Primary 1 for September 2018 - please remember we can accept up to 60 children.


Tuesday 15 May

Information Evening for parents (from 7.30pm to 9.00pm).


Tuesday 12 and Wednesday 13 June

Meet the Teacher afternoons for our new P1s. The boys and girls will have the opportunity to visit their classroom and to meet their P1 teacher. This begins at 2.10pm and finishes at 2.45pm.
