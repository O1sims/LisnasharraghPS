Wrap-Around Club

Breakfast Club

Time. 7:45AM to 8:45PM

Price. 3 pounds per morning

The Breakfast Club is available every morning from 7:45AM to 8:45AM for P1-P7 children and it is not necessary to book in advance. It costs Â£3 per day, 15 pounds for all week or 25 pounds per week for 2 children. It is located in the Play Based Learning Centre and is organised by three of the classroom assistants; Mrs Graham, Mrs Todd and Mrs Hall. For breakfast children can have cereal, pancakes, toast, yogurt, fruit, fruit juice, milk or water. After breakfast children can play with their friends and when the bell rings at 8:45AM children either walk or are dropped off to their classroom to begin lessons.


2-to-3 Club

Time. 2:00PM to 2:50PM

Price. 2.50 pounds per day

2 to 3 Club runs from 2:00PM to 2:50PM for P1, P2 and P3 children. It costs 2.50 pounds per day. Parents complete a weekly booking form on Thursday (for the following week) and send it in to your childs clas teacher with the correct money. When the bell goes at 2:00PM children make their way to the Play based Learning Centre where classroom assistants are waiting. Children can play games, take part in craft activities or if the weather is good play football in the school garden or go on the climbing frame. When it is time to go home parents can collect their children from the P1-P2 door.


Afternoon Club

Time. 2:50PM to 5:00PM

Price. 4 pounds per hour

The Afternoon Club runs from 2:50PM to 5:00PM for P1-P7 children. It costs Â£4 per each session and there is a 2 pounds reduction when your child is attending an after school activity. For this club, we ask parents to complete a form that covers the next four school weeks. Please note that it is essential to have bookings in advance so that we can arrange appropriate staffing levels. This is staffed by classroom assistants who provide a snack for the children and organise games, craft and cooking.


Booking forms

If you would like to book your child's place in the 2-to-3 Club and/or the Afternoon Club the forms will be given out in school or please feel free to call in to have a chat.
